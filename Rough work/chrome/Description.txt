Cannon Balls is made on the old school wars of cannons and castles. In those times, cannon was a very important addition to the wars to breach the walls of castles. It re-innovated the strategies of wars. This game has cartoon flavour of cannons and castles. There are two cannons which should be placed strategically to aim castles, towers and lights houses. This game brings out the power of cannons by mimicking fragility of castles. One precise single shot of both the cannons is all that is required to destroy all the edifices in the stage.
